Two Hierarchies / and they point opposite ways

Snowflake has two family trees, and interviewers love conflating them. One is about where things live. The other is about who can touch them. Confuse the direction and the whole model breaks.

"Objects nest downward. Roles inherit upward."

04 The Three Grants

Every access-control question in Snowflake reduces to one of these.

GRANT SELECT ON TABLE sat_claim_status TO ROLE analyst;
privilege → object → role. Gives a role a specific action on a specific object.
GRANT ROLE data_engineer TO ROLE sysadmin;
role → role. The "attach" move from Fig. 03 — folds a custom role into the inheritance tree.
GRANT ROLE analyst TO USER helanan;
role → person. Hands a human being everything that role can do.
