What just happened
Change any field and click Run Load. If the hashdiff of your new row matches the current satellite row's hashdiff, Snowflake skips the insert — nothing actually changed. If it differs, a new dated row gets inserted and becomes the current version. Try the "Replay the Ascension bug" button to see exactly what broke in your incident: a blank File Date passed straight into the satellite.
